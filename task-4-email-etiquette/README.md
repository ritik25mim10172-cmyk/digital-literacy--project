## Task 4 : Draft a Professional Email & Etiquette Guide

Poor digital communication can lead to misunderstandings and problems. For example, if a student sends an email without a proper subject line or uses informal language, it may seem unprofessional and disrespectful. This can result in a delayed or negative response from the recipient.

In such situations, proper communication practices should be followed, including using a clear subject, formal tone, and polite language. By following proper email etiquette, misunderstandings can be avoided and communication becomes more effective.
