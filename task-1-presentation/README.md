## Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

For this task, I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva, a user-friendly online design tool. The infographic explains the concept of digital literacy and highlights important aspects such as useful digital tools for students and safe internet practices. Digital literacy is defined as the ability to use digital technologies effectively, responsibly, and safely.

In the infographic, I included examples of commonly used tools like Google Docs and online communication platforms that help students in academic work. I also emphasized safe internet practices such as using strong passwords and avoiding suspicious links.

While designing the infographic, I focused on making it visually clear and engaging by using icons, colors, and short bullet points instead of long text. One challenge I faced was choosing the right layout and balancing text with visuals. Overall, this task helped me understand the importance of digital literacy and improved my ability to present information creatively.
